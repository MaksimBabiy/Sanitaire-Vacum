Evolution of Excellence: The Sanitaire Vacuum Story

Sanitaire Vacuum, a renowned brand in the cleaning industry, traces its roots back to its founding in 1972 by Charles W. Scales. From the beginning, the company's core values centered on delivering high-quality, durable, and efficient vacuum cleaners for commercial and industrial use. With a commitment to excellence, Sanitaire Vacuum quickly gained a reputation for reliability and superior cleaning performance. Over the years, Sanitaire Vacuum has continually evolved and improved its products to meet the ever-changing needs of its customers. One of the important milestones was the development of a motor with an extended service life, which provides more than 2,000 hours of operation in commercial cleaning mode, setting a new standard in the industry. The brand's dedication to innovation and convenient user functions, such as tool-free maintenance and large-capacity bags, has made Sanitaire Vacuum a trusted choice for businesses seeking reliable, long-lasting and highly productive cleaning systems. Today, Sanitaire Vacuum continues to uphold its legacy of excellence, delivering top-notch products that make commercial cleaning simpler and more effective.
